Common Car Damages & Repairs

Full Body Painting

Restoring the vehicle’s exterior by repainting the entire body, often needed after major scratches, fading, or accidents.

Dent Removal

Removing minor and major dents caused by collisions, hailstorms, or parking mishaps without repainting.

Bumper Repair

Fixing or replacing damaged bumpers due to accidents, improving the vehicle’s aesthetics and protection.

Scratch & Paint Touch-ups

Repairing minor scratches and chips in the car’s paint to maintain its appearance and prevent rusting.

Headlight & Taillight Repairs

Replacing or restoring broken, dim, or foggy headlights and taillights for better visibility and safety.

Our Process

Part of Our Working Process

01
Surface Preparation

The car is cleaned, sanded, and any dents or rust are repaired to create a smooth base.

02
Priming

A high-quality primer is applied to ensure better paint adhesion and long-lasting results.

03
Sanding & Smoothing

The primer is sanded down for a flawless surface before painting.

04
Painting & Clear Coating

Multiple paint coats ensure a vibrant finish, followed by a clear coat for shine and protection.
